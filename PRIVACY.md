# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how **WARC** collects, processes, stores, and protects information in connection with the operation of its proprietary, closed-source software service (the “Service”).

By accessing or using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Scope and Principles

This Privacy Policy applies solely to data processed as part of providing, operating, securing, and enforcing the WARC Service.

WARC does **not** operate backdoors, spyware, or covert surveillance mechanisms, and does **not** collect data unlawfully or beyond what is reasonably necessary to provide and protect the Service.

All data processing is conducted in accordance with applicable law and legitimate service interests.

---

## 2. Categories of Data We Process

### 2.1 Runtime and Service Diagnostics Data

WARC may automatically collect technical runtime data generated during use of the Service, including but not limited to:

- Error reports and crash logs  
- Runtime exceptions and fault diagnostics  
- Service stability and performance metrics  

This data is collected exclusively to:
- Diagnose and resolve technical issues  
- Maintain service reliability and integrity  
- Prevent service abuse or malfunction  

Such data does **not** include user keystrokes, screen content, personal files, or unrelated application data.

---

### 2.2 Cryptographic Hashes and Security Materials

WARC may store and process cryptographic data necessary for service security, including:

- Hashed identifiers  
- Associated salts  
- Integrity verification or validation values  

These materials are used solely for authentication, license enforcement, fraud prevention, and protection of proprietary systems.  
Plaintext secrets are not intentionally stored.

---

### 2.3 Transaction and Billing Records

For accounting, compliance, and dispute resolution purposes, WARC may retain limited transaction-related data, such as:

- Transaction identifiers  
- Purchase or subscription status  
- Timestamps and reference metadata  

WARC does **not** store payment card numbers, banking credentials, or sensitive financial information.

---

### 2.4 Device Fingerprinting and Hardware Identifiers

To enforce licensing and prevent unauthorized access, sharing, or abuse, WARC may collect and process technical device identifiers, including:

- Device fingerprints  
- Hardware identifiers (HWID)  
- Runtime or instance identifiers  

These identifiers are used strictly for:
- Access control  
- License validation  
- Abuse and fraud prevention  

They are **not** used for advertising, cross-service tracking, or unrelated profiling.

---

### 2.5 Runtime Monitoring for Abuse Prevention

To protect the Service, its users, and its intellectual property, WARC reserves the right to perform **limited and proportionate runtime monitoring**, which may include:

- Detection of abnormal execution patterns  
- Identification of unauthorized modification, tampering, or circumvention attempts  
- Detection of financial fraud, service abuse, or misuse  
- Protection against unauthorized analysis, extraction, or reverse engineering of proprietary components  

Such monitoring is strictly limited to what is necessary for service security and enforcement, and does **not** constitute intrusive surveillance or backdoor access.

---

### 2.6 Other Operational Data

WARC may process additional technical or operational data where reasonably necessary to:

- Operate and maintain the Service  
- Enforce contractual and license obligations  
- Detect security incidents or policy violations  
- Comply with applicable legal requirements  

---

## 3. Data We Do Not Collect

WARC does **not** collect or process:

- Real names, physical addresses, or phone numbers  
- Government-issued identifiers  
- Roblox account credentials or passwords  
- Keystrokes, screen captures, or content monitoring  
- Covert telemetry, spyware, or hidden data collection  

---

## 4. Purpose Limitation

All data is processed exclusively for:

- Service operation and maintenance  
- License enforcement and access control  
- Security, integrity, and abuse prevention  
- Fraud detection and dispute resolution  
- Legal compliance  

WARC does **not** process data for advertising, resale, behavioral profiling, or unrelated analytics.

---

## 5. Legal Basis for Processing

Depending on jurisdiction, WARC processes data under one or more of the following legal bases:

- Performance of a contract (service delivery and licensing)  
- Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, protection of intellectual property)  
- User consent, where explicitly required by law  

---

## 6. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

Where appropriate, data may be anonymized or deleted after expiration or termination of service access.

---

## 7. Data Security

WARC implements reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including:

- Hashing or irreversible transformation of sensitive identifiers  
- Access controls and internal restrictions  
- Segregation of licensing, runtime, and operational systems  

No system can be guaranteed to be fully secure.
